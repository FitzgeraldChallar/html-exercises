Paris – Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. Located in Paris, France, it attracts millions of tourists every year and represents romance, art, and architectural excellence.

Rome – Colosseum

The Colosseum in Rome is a historic amphitheater that dates back to ancient Roman times. It is known for gladiatorial contests and is a powerful symbol of the Roman Empire.

New York – Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty stands on Liberty Island in New York Harbor. It symbolizes freedom and democracy and is one of the most famous landmarks in the United States.
